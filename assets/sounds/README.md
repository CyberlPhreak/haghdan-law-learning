# Sound effects

These short PCM WAV cues were generated specifically for HaghDan and are bundled locally. The app layers separate player instances and delayed accents for richer correct-answer and milestone feedback:

- tap.wav: gentle interface tick and success accent
- correct-clap.wav: short synthetic applause and success shimmer
- incorrect.wav: soft descending retry cue without a harsh buzzer

They contain no third-party recordings or licensed samples.
