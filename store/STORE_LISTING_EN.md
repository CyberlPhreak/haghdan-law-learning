# Suggested Store Listing — English

## Name

HaghDān: Law and SQE in Persian

## Short description

Persian-first learning for the law of England and Wales, FLK1, FLK2 and SQE2 skills.

## Full description

HaghDān is an offline-first bilingual study companion designed for Persian speakers learning the law of England and Wales.

- Structured FLK1 and FLK2 subject coverage
- All six SQE2 practical skills
- Guided lessons with English legal terminology
- Applied scenarios, checklists and exam clinics
- Five-option practice bank
- Quick, diagnostic and timed 90-question sessions
- Spaced review, bookmarks, streaks and attempt history
- No account and no advertising

HaghDān is independent and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the SRA or Kaplan SQE. Content is general education, not legal advice. Practice results do not guarantee an examination pass.
